Molecular Microbiology & Immunology

Department members focus their research on understanding basic biological processes underlying the major infectious diseases affecting public health, including malaria, tuberculosis and AIDS.

To combat global health threats caused by parasites, bacteria and viruses, faculty and students conduct their studies in laboratories, in the field and in clinics.
